Make an impact. Get involved.

Help fuel the future by creating a chapter at your school! Not a student in high school or college? You can still get involved by creating a chapter and helping young adults at your local library, primary schools, or even youth groups!


Regardless of background, socioeconomic standing, education level, or knowledge of personal finance, Students for Financial Literacy is committed to giving as many people the opportunity to make an impact as possible. All that matters is your passion to make a difference in the world. We provide the framework to getting started and all you need is the passion to learn.


Why create a chapter?

1. We provide the resources you need to get started teaching about financial literacy, courtesy of the HSFFP.

2. You are able to be part of an organization much larger than just yourself, allowing you to make a much larger impact!

3. Our network of SFFL Chapter Leaders support each other and are able to give tips in growing your chapter and fundraising.

4. We make it easy to get started with your school's administration, with a guide regarding the steps most schools require to start a club

5. We were founded by students, for students! We have absolutely no fee to starting a chapter, as we feel that there should be no restrictions on someone who is trying to make a difference!
